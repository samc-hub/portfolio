About — Colophon

Formerly a registered nurse. Now a full-stack developer — building, shipping, and maintaining client platforms and health-tech tools for the web.

I'm a web developer at OCG Creative. I've built 20+ client sites from the ground up and maintain 60+ across WordPress, WooCommerce, Shopify, and Wix — owning custom development in JavaScript, PHP, SQL, HTML, and CSS. I've shipped a custom WooCommerce plugin approved for the official WordPress repository, migrated a 15,000-user store from Magento to WooCommerce, and delivered five e-commerce sites end to end including payment, shipping, and launch.

On contract with Vallige I built a Sanity-backed marketing site for a healthcare technology company — owning architecture through deployment, including tag-based ISR, signed webhooks, and a custom Studio for non-technical editors. Before tech I was a registered nurse, which is where the pull toward health-tech comes from. This August I start a master's in Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania.
